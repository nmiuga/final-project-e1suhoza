#  Overall Learnings
What I liked most about this experience for this project was having free reign to do whatever I wanted. I loved the idea of journaling about fun restaurants that I have been to. I learned a lot more about the process and writing different prompts. Eventually, I went with a simpler prompt because I liked the way that AI would lay it out, and I would not lose any of my ideas in the process. 
# Challenges
One of the large challenges that I faced was adding in the location feature. I had trouble figuring out how to allow it in the info.plist section of my work. Eventually, I got it to work and each user would just have to allow location settings for the app.
# Next Steps
My next steps after this project to expand on the app would be adding a gallery. I like the idea of a Pinterest looking gallery to display all of the photos of food. It would be a fun way to look back on all of the places I have been to eat and be able to scroll through and compare different foods.
